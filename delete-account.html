WasliGo - Account Deletion

If you would like to permanently delete your WasliGo account and all associated personal data, you may request account deletion at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to:

khader.sadam.hocine@gmail.com

Email Subject:

WasliGo Account Deletion Request

Please include:

  • Your full name.
  • Your registered phone number.
  • Your username (if available).

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account.
  • Profile information.
  • Personal information stored in WasliGo.
  • Related application data unless retention is required by law.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days after verification.
